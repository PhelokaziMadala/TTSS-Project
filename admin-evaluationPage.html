Score Explanations

Attendance Scoring

5 - Excellent: Always on time, never misses scheduled sessions, actively participates

4 - Good: Consistently on time with rare absences that are communicated in advance

3 - Average: Occasional tardiness or absences, generally communicates in advance

2 - Below Average: Frequent tardiness or absences affecting productivity

1 - Poor: Unreliable attendance with no communication

Communication Scoring

5 - Excellent: Articulates ideas clearly, listens actively, excellent written and verbal skills

4 - Good: Communicates well, responsive, generally clear in expression

3 - Average: Adequate communication skills, sometimes unclear but gets points across

2 - Below Average: Often unclear or unresponsive, communication issues impact work

1 - Poor: Significant communication barriers affecting all interactions

Accountability Scoring (Quiz-Based)

5 - Excellent: Consistently demonstrates strong accountability by scoring high on quizzes, showing thorough understanding and ownership of learning.

4 - Good: Shows good accountability with solid quiz scores and mostly reliable follow-through on tasks.

3 - Average: Meets basic expectations with average quiz results but occasionally misses details or follow-up actions.

2 - Below Average: Struggles with accountability, reflected in low quiz scores and inconsistent task completion.

1 - Poor: Demonstrates minimal accountability, poor quiz performance, and failure to complete assigned responsibilities.

Creativity and Ownership Scoring

5 - Excellent: Produces innovative and visually compelling project slides and GitHub repositories, consistently adding unique ideas and taking full ownership of design elements.

4 - Good: Shows creativity in project presentations and maintains well-organized GitHub repos with meaningful contributions.

3 - Average: Delivers adequate designs and project slides, with some creativity but relies on standard templates or existing examples.

2 - Below Average: Limited creativity or originality in design work; GitHub contributions are minimal or poorly structured.

1 - Poor: Displays little to no creative effort; project materials lack polish and GitHub repos show lack of ownership or contribution.

Project Delivery Scoring (Presentation Skills)

5 - Excellent: Presents projects clearly and confidently, engaging the audience with well-structured content and polished delivery.

4 - Good: Delivers presentations effectively with good clarity and organization, maintaining audience interest.

3 - Average: Presentation meets basic requirements but may lack smooth flow or confidence in delivery.

2 - Below Average: Presentation is disorganized or unclear, with weak delivery that affects audience understanding.

1 - Poor: Fails to communicate project details effectively; presentation is confusing or lacks professionalism.

Tech Skills Scoring

5 - Excellent: Demonstrates expert-level technical knowledge, quickly learns new tools, and mentors others

4 - Good: Solid technical skills, applies knowledge effectively, and adapts to new technologies

3 - Average: Adequate technical ability with occasional guidance needed

2 - Below Average: Limited technical skills that affect productivity and require frequent support

1 - Poor: Insufficient teInsufficient technical skills that hinder task completion and team progress
